Thalapathy Vijay Movies

1. Master (2021)

Vijay stars as JD, a troubled professor who is sent to a juvenile school, where he battles a criminal kingpin exploiting young students. This action thriller received widespread acclaim for its intense storyline and powerful performances.

2. Bigil (2019)

In this sports drama, Vijay plays a dual role as a former football player and his son, who coaches a women's football team. The film is known for its inspiring story and Vijay's strong performances in both roles.

3. Mersal (2017)

Vijay plays three roles in this action thriller about a doctor, a magician, and their fight against corruption in the medical field. The film combines action with social themes and became a major hit.
